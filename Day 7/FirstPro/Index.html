 DO'S AND DONT'S IN LABOURATORY  

  1. Make entry in the Log Book as soon as you enter the Labouratory
  2. All the students should sit according to their roll numbers starting from their left to right
  3. All the students are supposed to enter the terminal number in the log book.
  4. Do not change the terminal number in the log book.
  5. All the students are expected to get at least the algorithm of the program/concept to be implemented.
  6. Strictly observe the instructions given by teacher/Lab instructor.

 INSTRUCTIONS FOR LABOURATORY TEACHERS 

  1. Submission related to whatever lab work has been completed should be done during the next lab session. The immediate arrangements for prinouts related to submission on the day of practical assignments.
  2. Student should be taught for taking the printouts under the observation of lab teacher.
